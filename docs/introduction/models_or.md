## Choosing the Right Model

Consider these factors when selecting an AI model:

1. **Task Requirements**: What specific tasks do you need to accomplish?
2. **Context Length**: How much information do you need to process at once?
3. **Multimodal Needs**: Do you need image, audio, or video processing?
4. **Speed vs. Quality**: Balance between response time and output quality
5. **Cost**: API pricing and usage limits
6. **Integration**: Compatibility with your existing tools and workflows
7. **Language Support**: Multilingual capabilities if needed
